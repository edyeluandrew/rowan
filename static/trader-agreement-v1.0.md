# ROWAN OTC TRADER AGREEMENT — v1.0

**Effective Date:** Upon acceptance  
**Parties:** You ("Trader") and Rowan ("Platform")

---

## WHO THIS IS FOR

This agreement is between you — an independent mobile money trader — and Rowan, a platform that connects people who want to convert cryptocurrency (USDC) to mobile money. By accepting this agreement, you confirm that you understand the rules below and agree to follow them.

---

## 1. YOUR ROLE

You are an **independent OTC liquidity provider**. When Rowan matches you with a cash-out request:

1. You will receive a notification with the amount and mobile money network.
2. You **accept or decline** the request within the allowed time.
3. If you accept, you send the mobile money amount to the recipient's phone number.
4. Once confirmed, Rowan releases the USDC to your Stellar wallet.

You are **not** an employee, agent, or partner of Rowan. You operate your own float, at your own risk.

---

## 2. WHAT YOU MUST HAVE

Before you can receive your first match, you must:

- [x] Provide a **valid government-issued ID** (national ID or passport).
- [x] Verify ownership of at least **one mobile money account** via OTP.
- [x] Show proof of **Binance P2P trading history** (screenshot of your profile stats).
- [x] Have a funded **Stellar wallet** with a USDC trustline.
- [x] Declare your **available fiat float** honestly.

---

## 3. HOW YOU EARN

For each completed transaction:

- You buy USDC at the **wholesale rate** set by Rowan.
- You send fiat at the **payout amount** shown in the request.
- The difference between the wholesale rate and the user-facing rate is Rowan's spread. Your margin comes from acquiring USDC below your wholesale rate (e.g., through Binance P2P or OTC channels).

Rowan does **not** charge you a fee per transaction. Your cost is the USDC you acquire; your revenue is the wholesale USDC you receive.

---

## 4. RULES YOU MUST FOLLOW

### Timely Payouts
- You must send mobile money **within the confirm window** (currently 5 minutes after accepting).
- If you fail to confirm on time, the request will be reassigned and your trust score will decrease.

### Honest Reporting
- Report your float accurately. If you accept a request you cannot fulfill, your account may be suspended.
- Do not inflate your Binance P2P statistics or provide falsified documents.

### One Account Per Person
- Each trader must use their own verified identity. Sharing accounts or credentials is prohibited.

### No Front-Running
- Do not use information from Rowan requests to trade ahead of users on other platforms.

### Compliance
- You must comply with the laws of your country regarding mobile money transfers and digital asset trading.
- Rowan may ask you for additional verification at any time.

---

## 5. YOUR TRUST SCORE

Rowan assigns a trust score (0-100) to every trader. Your score goes **up** with successful, timely completions and **down** when:

- You decline requests frequently.
- You fail to confirm payouts on time.
- A dispute is resolved against you.
- You go offline during peak hours after declaring high float.

Traders with higher trust scores receive matches first. If your score drops below a threshold set by Rowan, you will stop receiving matches until it recovers.

---

## 6. SUSPENSION AND BANNING

Rowan may **suspend** your account immediately if:

- You fail to send mobile money after accepting a request.
- A user files a dispute and evidence shows you did not pay.
- Your documents are found to be falsified.
- You are suspected of fraud, money laundering, or sanctions evasion.

Suspension is **not permanent** — you may appeal by contacting Rowan support. However, repeated violations will result in a **permanent ban**.

---

## 7. DISPUTES

If a user claims they did not receive mobile money:

1. Rowan will open a dispute and notify you.
2. You must provide proof of payment (mobile money receipt, transaction confirmation) within **24 hours**.
3. Rowan's admin team will review and resolve the dispute.
4. If resolved against you, the USDC for that transaction may be withheld and your trust score will decrease.

---

## 8. YOUR DATA

Rowan stores:
- Your name, email, and phone hash (not the actual phone number).
- Your government ID (front, back) and selfie — used only for verification.
- Your Binance P2P profile screenshot — used only for verification.
- Your Stellar wallet address and transaction history on the platform.

Rowan does **not** sell your data. Documents are stored in a private, encrypted bucket and are accessible only to Rowan administrators during verification and dispute resolution.

---

## 9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

- Rowan is a matching platform. We do not hold your fiat or control mobile money networks.
- Rowan is not liable for mobile money network outages, Stellar network congestion, or losses caused by your own errors (e.g., sending to the wrong number).
- Rowan's maximum liability for any single transaction is the USDC amount of that transaction.

Rowan may update this agreement from time to time. If we make material changes:

- You will be notified via the platform.
- You must accept the new version to continue receiving matches.
- If you do not accept, your account will be paused (not suspended) until you do.
